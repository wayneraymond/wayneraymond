BENSONS FOR BEDS

Bensons for Beds is a national bed retailer offering famous brand name beds at low prices. After working closely with them to refresh their website, we began to develop a tablet app that would extend the customer experience and generate further online sales.

The app is designed to guide the customer through the buying process and suggest appropriate products to match their criteria, this may be based on comfort ratings, budgets or payment plans, allowing them to 'buy their perfect bed wherever they are.'

Project status: In planning
